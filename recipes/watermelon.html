Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Watermelon
  • 2 Cucumbers
  • 1 Lime
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Chili Powder

Steps:

  1. Chop and cube watermelon, discarding the rinds.
  2. Slice cucumber to desired thickness and halve.
  3. In a bowl combine along with salt, chili powder and pepper. Mix well.
  4. After mixing, squeeze the juice of two lime halves over the salad. Squeeze across salad to ensure it reaches all corners. Serve and enjoy!
