Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup finely grated cucumber
  • 1 cup thick whole milk greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped mint, optional

Steps:

  1. Place the cucumber on a towel and gently squeeze out a bit of the excess water.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the cucumber, yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, dill, and mint (optional). Chill until ready
